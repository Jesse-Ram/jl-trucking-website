Our Equipment

Box Truck Overview

JL Trucking operates a well-maintained box truck capable of handling general freight, moving loads, furniture removal, and commercial hauling throughout Southern California.

Our enclosed box truck provides a clean, secure cargo area suitable for a wide range of loads — from palletized freight and household goods to large furniture and business equipment. Every load is handled with proper securement and care.

Brokers, shippers, and customers can use the specifications on this page to verify that your load fits our truck before booking. If you have a load that's close to our limits or you're not sure it qualifies, call 951-833-8122 and we'll confirm right away.

Operating under MC# 980145 and DOT# 2807485, JL Trucking is fully licensed and insured for every job.
