What We Do

Professional Transportation Services
You Can Rely On

JL Trucking provides a full range of carrier, logistics, moving, and removal services across Southern California. Whatever you need moved, we've got the experience, equipment, and commitment to get it done right.

Carrier Services

JL Trucking operates as a licensed motor carrier under MC# 980145, providing reliable general freight and cargo hauling for brokers, businesses, and individuals. Whether you need a single load transported or ongoing carrier support, we offer dependable service with professional communication throughout the entire process.

We understand that on-time performance and cargo safety are non-negotiable in the trucking industry. That's why we treat every load as if it were our own.

General Freight Box Truck Hauling Licensed MC Carrier On-Time Performance

Logistics Services

Effective logistics is about more than just driving a truck — it's about coordinating every step of the transportation process to ensure your freight or cargo arrives where it needs to be, when it needs to be there. JL Trucking offers logistics support including scheduling, route planning, pickup coordination, and delivery management.

Whether you're managing a one-time shipment or need reliable logistics support for your business, we're here to simplify the process and keep things moving efficiently.

Scheduling Route Coordination Pickup & Delivery Freight Management

Moving Services

Moving is stressful enough without having to worry about transportation. JL Trucking provides professional moving services for residential and commercial customers across Southern California. We handle your items with care, arrive on schedule, and ensure everything makes it safely to your new location.

From apartment moves to full household relocations, from small office moves to commercial equipment transport — JL Trucking brings the professionalism and reliability your move deserves.

Residential Moving Commercial Moving Apartment Moves Office Relocation

Large Furniture Removal

Getting rid of large, bulky furniture items can be a major hassle — but not when you have JL Trucking on your side. We provide fast, efficient pickup and removal of large furniture items for homeowners, landlords, property managers, and businesses. No item is too big or too heavy.

We remove couches, sofas, sectionals, mattresses, box springs, dining tables, desks, office furniture, cabinets, appliances, and more. Schedule your removal today and we'll haul it away so you don't have to.

Couch & Sofa Removal Mattress Removal Office Furniture Appliances Bulky Items

Local Trucking

Need a reliable truck for a local or regional haul? JL Trucking specializes in local trucking services for customers throughout the Inland Empire and Southern California. We're flexible, responsive, and ready to take on jobs of all sizes — from small loads to large deliveries.

Local trucking clients include contractors, retailers, wholesalers, warehouses, and individual customers who need dependable, affordable hauling without the hassle of dealing with a large, impersonal carrier.

Inland Empire Same-Day Available Flexible Scheduling All Load Sizes

Freight Transportation

JL Trucking provides professional general freight transportation for businesses and individuals who need their goods moved safely and efficiently. Operating a clean, well-maintained box truck, we handle palletized and loose general freight with proper loading, securement, and timely delivery to the destination.

Working with freight brokers, businesses, and direct customers, we offer competitive rates and consistent, professional service on every haul. Our DOT compliance and federal licensing mean you can count on us for freight that requires a licensed, regulated carrier. Please note: we handle general freight only — no hazmat and no refrigerated (reefer) loads.

General Freight Only Palletized Loads DOT Compliant Broker Friendly

Residential & Commercial Hauling

JL Trucking provides general hauling services for both residential and commercial customers. Whether you need items hauled away from a property, materials delivered to a job site, or excess inventory moved from one location to another, we have the truck and the manpower to make it happen.

Our residential and commercial hauling services are ideal for homeowners cleaning out a garage or storage unit, contractors needing materials moved, landlords dealing with tenant cleanouts, and businesses managing facility changes.
