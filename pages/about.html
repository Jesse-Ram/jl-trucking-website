About JL Trucking

Our Story

Driven by Dedication,
Built on Trust

JL Trucking is a licensed and insured motor carrier based in Highland, California, owned and operated by Jorge A. Lopez Perez, doing business as Jorge Lopez Trucking. Founded with a commitment to professional, dependable transportation, JL Trucking has grown to serve residential customers, businesses, and freight brokers throughout the greater Inland Empire and Southern California.

Jorge built JL Trucking on a foundation of hard work, honest business practices, and a genuine commitment to his customers. Every load, every move, and every delivery is treated with the same level of care and professionalism — because Jorge understands that when you trust someone with your belongings or your freight, that trust matters.

We believe great trucking is about more than just moving cargo from point A to point B. It's about showing up on time, communicating clearly, handling your property with respect, and standing behind our work. That's what JL Trucking does every day.
