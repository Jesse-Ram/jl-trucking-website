How We Price

Custom Quotes for Every Job

Every trucking and logistics job is different. That's why JL Trucking doesn't use one-size-fits-all pricing. Instead, we build a custom quote based on the specific details of your job — so you only pay for exactly what you need.

Distance

Local, regional, and long-distance rates vary. Tell us where pickup and drop-off are located.

Load Size & Weight

The volume and weight of your shipment are primary factors in determining your rate.

Type of Service

Carrier, logistics, moving, furniture removal, and freight services are each priced accordingly.

Labor Required

Jobs requiring loading, unloading, or extra hands are priced to reflect the labor involved.

Pickup & Drop-Off Location

Access, stairs, floor level, and location type can affect total job cost.

Scheduling & Availability

Rush jobs, weekend availability, and peak scheduling windows may affect pricing.

Our Pricing Promise

JL Trucking believes in honest, transparent pricing. We will never charge hidden fees or surprise you with unexpected costs after the job. Your quote is your price — and we stand behind it.
